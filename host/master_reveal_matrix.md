## 🛠️ 3. Host Action Checklist
1. **Choose the Killer** (Roll D100 or Pick your favorite actor/guest).
2. **Hide the Evidence**: Use the "Find" location listed in the Character Profile.
3. **Drop the Clues**: Ensure the "Red Herring" clues are distributed to other players via the Clue Database.
4. **The Final Toast**: After the Second Body is discovered, call for the Final Reveal to start the showdown.
