# Murder Mystery Funny Objectives & Mechanics

## Funny Objectives (Assign to Characters)

These are secret goals characters can pursue during the party. They make interactions hilarious and give everyone something to do besides just solve the murder!

### Romance/Relationship Chaos
- **Seduce [specific person] for their inheritance** (assign to Gentleman George or Lorenzo)
- **Convince someone you're dating their ex** (create chaos!)
- **Maintain TWO secret relationships without getting caught** (Dorothy's objective)
- **Get romantically rejected by as many people as possible** (track rejections, wear them like a badge)

### Competitive/Petty Goals
- **Win the shuffleboard tournament despite being terrible** (cheat, make up rules)
- **Hide the TV remote and watch the chaos unfold** (extra points if someone accuses someone else)
- **Steal the "good blanket" from the common room** (everyone wants this blanket)
- **Get someone else to take your medication for you** (convince them it's candy)
- **Win BINGO before the murder happens** (competitive bingo!)

### Social Manipulation
- **Get someone caught sneaking alcohol into their room** (plant evidence)
- **Convince someone they forgot to sign in** (Diane will freak out)
- **Get someone banned from the dining hall** (complain about their table manners)
- **Expose someone's secret affair** (increase the drama!)
- **Make everyone think someone else is losing their memory** (gaslight kindly)

### Investigation Interference
- **Lead the investigation to the WRONG conclusion** (great for the killer!)
- **Get yourself accused of the murder** (chaotic neutral energy)
- **Take credit for solving the murder even if you didn't** (Detective Marge's objective)
- **Plant false evidence against someone you dislike**
- **Declare the death "Natural Causes" and try to move on** (Staff objective: Avoid paperwork)

### Ridiculous Goals
- **Get everyone to complain about the thermostat** (make it a movement)
- **Convince someone that "back in your day" something ridiculous was true**
- **Fall asleep during the murder reveal**  (commit to the bit)
- **Collect as many Werther's Originals as possible** (hoard them)
- **Get someone to attend one of Gerald's activities** (impossible challenge)

### Staff-Specific "Professional" Objectives
- **Solve the murder before a single resident does** (The "Professional Pride" goal)
- **Expose a resident's "illegal" activity** (Rose's gambling ring, or unauthorized pets)
- **"Clean Up" the crime scene before Marge arrives** (Apathy is key!)
- **Collect "Bribes" for every clue you give** (The most profitable staff member wins!)
- **Force the killer to admit it while doing Chair Cardio**

## Elderly Quirk Distribution

Mix and match these quirks among characters who don't have them assigned yet!

### Memory & Confusion
- Constantly asking "What year is it?"
- Mixing up other residents' names (calling people by random names)
- Repeating the same story 3 times in one conversation
- Confusing people with residents who died years ago
- "Have you seen my glasses?" (while wearing them)

### Hearing Problems
- Hearing aids that whistle loudly at random/dramatic moments
- Mishearing everything as something scandalous
- "WHAT?!" after every sentence
- Pretending not to hear inconvenient questions

### Physical Comedy
- Walker/cane as multipurpose tool (weapon, hook things, trip people)
- Moving very slowly but with surprising determination when motivated
- Falling asleep mid-conversation then waking up with perfect timing
- Adjusting hearing aids constantly (feedback squeals)

### Personality Quirks
- Asking "Have you eaten? You look thin" to everyone
- Complaining about the thermostat
- Talking about their "conditions" in graphic medical detail
- Mentioning "back in my day..." before everything
- Insisting they "don't trust computers" while trying to use them
- Offering hard candies from mysterious purse/pockets

## Natural Clue Collection Mechanics

### NPC Interaction Requirements

**Easy NPCs** (give clues readily):
- **Nurse Margaret**: Just let her check your blood pressure
- **Diane**: Sign the sign-in sheet and she'll talk your ear off

**Medium NPCs** (need proper approach):
- **Gerald**: Pretend to be interested in chair yoga OR ask about his clipboard
- **Rose**: Speak LOUDLY and call her "dearie" back

**Hard NPCs** (require persistence):
- **Frank**: Must ask at least 3 times, wait patiently, speak directly into his hearing aid
- **Sophia**: Catch her while she's awake (host discretion)

### Location-Based Clues

**Interactive Locations** (physically visit these areas):
- **Medication Room**: Find the empty vial
- **Bingo Hall**: Examine the victim's bingo card
- **Front Desk**: Read the sign-in sheet with Diane's "help"
- **Activity Room**: Check Gerald's attendance records
- **Nurse's Station**: Review the medication log

### The "Memory Malfunction" Mechanic

Some clues are intentionally WRONG due to elderly memory issues:
- Rose heard "You'll PAY for this" but it was actually "Do you want to PLAY for this?"
- Dorothy saw "Harold's ghost" but it was someone in a cardigan
- Detective Marge's "detailed notes" contradict each other

This makes the investigation funnier and more challenging!

## Funny Murder Methods (Expanded)

### Primary Methods
1. **Poisoned prune juice** - "Nature's remedy turned deadly!"
2. **Tampered blood pressure medication** - Gave them LOW pressure instead of lowering it
3. **Spiked early bird special meatloaf** - "It was already suspicious!"
4. **Poisoned bingo dauber ink** - Victim licked it for luck (as is tradition)
5. **Tainted Werther's Original candies** - The front desk bowl betrayal
6. **Spiked denture cream** - Diabolical and oddly specific

### Alternative Methods (If Host Wants Options)
7. **Poisoned Ensure shake** - The breakfast betrayal
8. **Deadly heating pad** - "For your back pain..." (too dark?)
9. **Spiked arthritis cream** - Applied topically, absorbed fatally
10. **Poisoned sugar-free treats** - Diabetic-friendly murder
