# Shady Pines Scoring Rules & Win Conditions

Make the murder mystery dinner even more engaging with a multi-layered scoring system that rewards different playstyles!

---

## 🏆 Multiple Win Conditions

Players can win in different ways, encouraging varied playstyles:

1. **Best Player** (highest points) - The master sleuth
2. **Best Dressed** (voted by group) - Most committed to character costume
3. **Best Actor** (voted by group) - Most entertaining performance/quirks

This means everyone has a chance to win something!

---

## 📊 Point System Breakdown

### Information Gathering (Clue Collection)
- **0.5 points per clue discovered**
- Encourages talking to NPCs and exploring locations
- Both narrative clues (from conversations) and physical clues count
- Maximum: ~15-20 clues available = 7.5-10 pts possible

### Resource Management (Tokens)
- **1 point per 5 tokens at end of game**
- Tokens are used in Rose's gambling ring or to "bribe" NPCs for information
- Creates tension: spend tokens for clues OR hoard for points?
- Starting tokens: 10 per player
- Ways to earn more: completing mini-objectives, winning at Rose's poker table

### Character Objectives (Personalized Goals)
- **1-3 points per objective completed**
- Each character has 2-3 secret objectives (from their bio)
- Examples:
  - Dorothy: Keep both relationships secret = 2 pts
  - Rose: Sell 3 pieces of gossip = 1 pt each (3 pts total)
  - Lorenzo: Successfully flirt with 4 people = 1 pt each (4 pts total)
  - Gentleman George: Get marriage proposal = 3 pts

### Active Accusation
- **5 points for making the first formal accusation**
- Encourages risk-taking
- Must present at least 2 pieces of evidence
- Only one player can claim this bonus

### Voting Correctly
- **3 points for voting for the correct killer**
- Rewards good reasoning and deduction
- Everyone submits a vote at the end

### Cause of Death Bonus
- **5 points for correctly identifying the murder method**
- Secondary puzzle beyond "whodunit"
- Host reveals 3-4 possible methods, players must deduce which one
- Can vote for killer AND method separately

### The Murderer's Dilemma
- **10 points if the murderer successfully frames someone else**
- If someone else gets the most votes, the killer gets the bonus
- **-5 points if correctly identified as the murderer**
- Makes being the murderer FUN and strategic
- The killer has real incentive to deflect and mislead

## 🎲 Token Economy System

### Starting Resources
- Each player starts with **10 tokens**
- Tokens represented by poker chips, candies, or printed "Shady Pines Bucks"

### Ways to Earn Tokens
- **Complete mini-objective**: +2 tokens
- **Win at Rose's poker table**: +3-5 tokens
- **Sell information to Rose**: +1 token per secret shared
- **Get someone to admit something**: +1 token
- **Successfully achieve funny objective** (steal blanket, hide remote): +2 tokens

### Ways to Spend Tokens
- **Bribe NPCs for extra clues**: 2-3 tokens per premium clue
- **Buy false information to plant**: 3 tokens
- **Gamble with Rose**: 1 token buy-in
- **"Hire" Frank to investigate someone**: 4 tokens
- **Access restricted location** (Director's Office): 3 tokens

### End-Game Conversion
- **Every 5 tokens = 1 point**
- Creates strategic choice: spend for information or save for points?

## 🎭 Host Implementation Guide

### Setup (Before Party)
1. **Print scoring sheets** for each player
2. **Prepare tokens** (poker chips, Monopoly money, or print custom "Shady Pines Bucks")
3. **Assign character objectives** (already in character bios)
4. **Create "Clue Cards"** that players can collect (checkboxes on tracking sheet)

### During Phase 1-2 (Before Murder)
- Players can earn tokens through mini-objectives
- Rose's gambling ring is active (quick poker hands or coin flips)
- NPCs can be bribed with tokens for bonus clues

### During Phase 4 (Investigation)
- Track who discovers which clues
- Players can spend tokens strategically
- Remind players they can save tokens for end-game points

### During Phase 5 (Accusation)
- **First person to formally accuse** gets 5 pts (must present evidence!)
- Collect votes for:
  1. Who is the killer?
  2. What was the murder method?
- Tally votes

### After Revelation
- **Score calculation** (5-10 minutes)
- Award "Best Player" (highest points)
- Vote for "Best Dressed" and "Best Actor"
- Announce all three winners!

## 🎯 Why This System Works

### Prevents Information Hoarding
- Tokens force choices: share info for tokens OR hoard clues for points
- Rose's character encourages information trading

### Rewards Different Playstyles
- **Investigators**: Focus on clue collection (10+ pts possible)
- **Actors**: Focus on quirks and objectives for voting wins
- **Strategists**: Manage tokens carefully (maximize end-game points)
- **Risk-takers**: Make first accusation, gamble with Rose

### Makes Being the Murderer Fun
- 10-point bonus for framing someone is tempting
- Encourages active deception vs. passive hiding
- -5 penalty isn't devastating (can still win on other metrics)

### Keeps Everyone Engaged
- Even if you're bad at solving, you can win on acting or objectives
- Multiple winners means more people feel successful
- Token economy creates micro-goals throughout

---

## 🎨 Optional Variations

### Competitive Mode
- Only "Best Player" wins
- All 3 categories count as one mega-score

### Collaborative Mode
- Everyone vs. the murderer
- If group identifies killer correctly, everyone but murderer wins
- Murderer wins if they escape detection

### Chaos Mode
- Double all point values
- Add random events that give/take tokens
- "Sophia woke up and is changing her will" = everyone loses 2 tokens

## 📊 Sample Scoring Scenario

**Player: Sarah as Dorothy**
- Discovered 8 clues = 4 pts
- Completed "Keep relationships secret" = 2 pts  
- Made first accusation = 5 pts
- Voted correctly = 3 pts
- Remaining tokens: 12 ÷ 5 = 2.4 pts (rounded to 2)
- **TOTAL: 16 points**

**Player: Mike as Lorenzo (the murderer)**
- Discovered 6 clues = 3 pts
- Successfully flirted with 4 people = 4 pts
- Framed someone else (most votes went to Dorothy!) = 10 pts
- Remaining tokens: 8 ÷ 5 = 1.6 pts (rounded to 1)
- **TOTAL: 18 points** (The murderer escapes AND wins!)
