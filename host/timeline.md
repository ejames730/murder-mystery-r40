# Host Timeline & Event Script - Shady Pines Murder Mystery

Use this as your master guide for running the dinner party.

---

## PRE-GAME SETUP (Before Guests Arrive)

### Host Preparation
- [ ] Choose the victim (can be a resident or Gerald the Activities Coordinator)
- [ ] Choose the murder method (see `story/the_murder.md` for options)
- [ ] Decide on the killer (or let it emerge organically)
- [ ] Print character bios and place at seats OR hand out on arrival
- [ ] Set up "locations" (use signs: "Medication Room," "Bingo Hall," "Front Desk," etc.)
- [ ] Prepare physical clue props (medication vial, sign-in sheet, bingo card, notes)

### Character Assignments
- **8 Primary Characters** (guests who want main roles)
- **4 NPCs** (guests comfortable with supporting/clue-carrier roles)
- **The Birthday Girl** gets her character bio with the party theme
- **The Host** plays the Administrator/Announcer

---

## PHASE 1: Arrivals & "Admission" (20-30 minutes)

### 🎭 OPENING SCENE
**Host (as Administrator):**  
*"Welcome to Shady Pines Retirement Community! We have a very special new resident joining us today—[Wife's Name], who is celebrating her FORTIETH birthday! Let's all make her feel... at home."*

### Activities
- Guests receive character bios
- Encourage "checking in" with Diane at the Front Desk (sign-in sheet!)
- Gerald tries to get people excited about chair yoga
- Residents complain about the thermostat, talk about their conditions
- NPCs mingle and start dropping character quirks

### Host Tips
- Encourage exaggerated elderly behavior (repeating stories, hearing problems, etc.)
- Play upbeat "retirement home music" (Lawrence Welk, big band, elevator music)

---

## PHASE 2: BINGO NIGHT Begins (30-45 minutes)

### The Setup
**Gerald (Activities Coordinator):**  
*"Alright my groovy cats and kittens! It's time for CHAMPIONSHIP BINGO! Don't be squares—let's get RADICAL with those daubers!"*

### Activities During Bingo
- Actually play 1-2 rounds of bingo (quick rounds!)
- Residents share their clues naturally while playing:
  - "Did you see...?"
  - "I overheard the strangest thing..."
  - Rose speaks VERY LOUDLY about her clues
  - Sophia falls asleep mid-conversation
  - Dorothy mentions Harold's bingo strategy
- NPCs circulate with their information (must be prompted properly)

### Natural Clue Collection
Players should be collecting clues by:
- Talking to NPCs (following their requirements)
- Visiting "locations" to find physical clues
- Eavesdropping on conversations
- Following up on suspicious behavior

---

## PHASE 3: THE MURDER! 🚨 (The Big Acting Spurt)

### 🎭 THE DISCOVERY
**Timing**: Right as someone calls "BINGO!" or after 2 rounds

**What Happens**:
- A scream rings out!
- [The Victim] is found slumped over at their bingo table (or face-first in meatloaf if at dinner)
- Bingo dauber still in hand (if that's the murder method)

**Nurse Margaret runs over:**  
*"OH MY! SWEETIE! CAN. YOU. HEAR. ME?! Someone call... wait, we're all already here. THIS PERSON IS DECEASED!"*

**Host (Administrator):**  
*"Everyone remain calm! Nobody leaves Shady Pines until we figure out what happened! This is a CODE... uh... what color are we for murder? Never mind. Nobody move!"*

---

## PHASE 4: Investigation (45-60 minutes)

### The Interrogation Phase
- Detective Marge immediately takes charge (or tries to)
- Players can:
  - Interview each other
  - Visit locations to find physical clues
  - Re-question NPCs (who may remember NEW details)
  - Form theories and alliances

### 🎭 ACTING SPURT #2 - The Argument
**Timing**: Midway through investigation

**Suggested Scene**: Dorothy's boyfriends discover each other
- Bernie: "Dorothy! Who is THIS?!"
- Sal: "I could ask YOU the same question, pal!"
- Dorothy: "Harold never had to deal with this kind of drama!"

**Alternative**: Sophia changes her will publicly
- "I'm leaving everything to... the houseplant. At least it doesn't ask for handouts."

### Host Role During Investigation
- Keep time moving
- Prompt NPCs to share clues if players are stuck
- Create urgency: "We need to solve this before the authorities arrive!"
- Drop hints if needed

---

## PHASE 5: The Accusation (15-20 minutes)

### The Final Reveal
**Host:**  
*"The time has come. Detective Marge, I believe you wanted to share your findings? Or perhaps... our Birthday Girl has solved her first Shady Pines mystery?"*

### Structure
1. **Each player names their suspect** and gives one piece of evidence
2. **Vote or host decides** based on evidence presented
3. **The killer makes their confession** (dramatic!)
   - OR the Birthday Girl/Detective Marge dramatically reveals the killer

### 🎭 ACTING SPURT #3 - The Confession
**The Killer:**  
*"Alright, you got me! I did it! And I would've gotten away with it too, if it weren't for you meddling retirees! They [insert motive]! What choice did I have?!"*

**Sophia wakes up:**  
*"I knew it was them all along. *yawn* Is bingo over yet?"*

---

## PHASE 6: Celebration! (Remainder of party)

### Transition Back to Birthday Party
**Host:**  
*"Well, we've solved one mystery tonight. The other mystery is how [Wife's Name] is ONLY 40! Let's celebrate!"*

### Activities
- Break character (or stay in character if it's funnier)
- Cake time!
- "Awards" for:
  - Best Detective
  - Best Quirk Performance
  - Most Suspicious Behavior
  - Best "Eh?" (if someone played Frank)

---

## HOST QUICK REFERENCE

### If Players Get Stuck
- Have NPCs volunteer information unprompted
- Gerald can check his attendance sheet: "According to my radical records..."
- Frank finally speaks up after being asked once: "Oh yeah, I saw the whole thing."
- Sophia wakes up with a perfect clue

### If It's Going Too Fast
- Add more bingo rounds
- Have more "acting spurts" (arguments, secret reveals)
- Make NPCs harder to crack (Frank needs 5 asks instead of 3)

### If It's Going Too Slow
- Host announces: "You have 15 minutes before the authorities arrive!"
- Speed up clue distribution
- Have Detective Marge call everyone together for "final statements"
