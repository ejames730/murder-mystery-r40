# Frank "The Ear" Morrison - Handyman

**Age**: 67
**Role**: NPC / Staff Member
**Occupation**: Handyman at Shady Pines (Former Investigative Journalist).

## Profile
Frank mumbles constantly, is genuinely hard of hearing, and says "Eh?" after every sentence. He moves at the pace of cold molasses. But Frank isn't just a slow-moving repairman; he's a man who spent forty years digging up secrets for the *Chicago Tribune*. He "retired" to Shady Pines to live a quiet life after one too many threats from the people he exposed. He views the residents not as seniors, but as subjects in a lifelong study of human nature.

## Quirks
- Mumbles incoherently (actually he's often reciting facts about the person he's looking at).
- "Eh? What's that now?" (Used to buy time while he processes information).
- Moves incredibly slowly with his toolbox (which contains a high-end digital recorder hidden among the wrenches).
- Adjusts his hearing aid constantly (it’s actually a sophisticated listening device he modified himself).
- Pretends not to understand questions to see if people will reveal more than they intended.
- Whistles "The Entertainer" when he's found a particularly juicy piece of gossip.
