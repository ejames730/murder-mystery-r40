# Eleanor "The Enigma" Vance

**Age**: 75
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: High-Society Widow / Secret Agent? (Rumored).

## Profile
Eleanor is the most elegant resident. She wears pearls even to breakfast and speaks in a low, conspiratorial whisper. She has a collection of ancient-looking artifacts in her room.

## Quirk
She never gives a straight answer. Everything is a riddle or a "state secret." She often pretends to be deaf to avoid unwanted questions.
