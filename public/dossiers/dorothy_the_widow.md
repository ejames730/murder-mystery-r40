# Dorothy "The Widow" Morrison

**Age**: 78
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: High School Principal

## Profile
Dorothy is elegant, sharp-witted, and mentions her "late husband Harold" approximately 47 times per conversation. Harold was apparently an expert on everything from shuffleboard strategy to proper denture maintenance. What Dorothy doesn't mention is that she's currently dating TWO residents: Bernie (the sweet one with the good hearing aid) and Sal (the suave former salesman).

## Quirks
- "Harold always said..." (before every piece of advice)
- Constantly adjusting her pearls when nervous
- Has a different story about how Harold died depending on who asks
- Keeps a framed photo of Harold on her walker (it's actually a stock photo)
