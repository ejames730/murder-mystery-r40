# Mr. James Tasker - Retirement Home Director

**Age**: 62
**Role**: Host / Administrator / Potential Suspect
**Personality**: Stressed, Frugal, and Secretly Soft-Hearted.

## Profile
As the Director of Shady Pines, you are the face of an institution that is slowly sinking. Your reputation for being "frugal"—buying generic denture cream and lowering the heat—isn't born of greed, but of a desperate, late-night battle with a spreadsheet that refuses to balance. You’re trying to save Shady Pines from being bought out by "The Silver Horizon," a corporate chain that would triple the residents' rent. You view the residents as a collection of difficult, expensive, and deeply beloved grandparents you are failing to protect.

## Your Background
You came to Shady Pines expecting a quiet end-of-career post. Instead, you found a facility crumbling under decades of mismanagement. You’ve been working sixteen-hour days, often doing the laundry yourself to save on staffing costs. You hired a "budget" professional investigator because you were desperate to find out who was sabotaging the facility’s equipment, but as usual, you tried to save money and ended up with a headache.

## Your Quirks
- **Frugal to a Fault**: You constantly mention costs ("Do you know the price of wholesale Jell-O? It's scandalous!"), but it’s a nervous tic.
- **The "Director" Voice**: You use a booming, authoritative voice to hide the fact that your hands are shaking.
- **Micro-Manager**: You obsess over things like missing TV remotes because they are the only things you feel you *can* control.
- **Hidden Sweets**: You carry a stash of high-quality chocolates to give to residents when they’re upset, but you always claim they were "donated."
