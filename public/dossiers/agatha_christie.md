# Agatha "The Plot" Christie

**Age**: 82
**Role**: NPC (Secondary Resident)
**Past Occupation**: Mystery Novelist (Author of *The Denture of Destiny*).

## Profile
Agatha doesn't say much, but her eyes are constantly scanning the room for "plot points." She views everyone as a character in a first-draft manuscript. She’s the facility’s resident expert on "how it’s always the person you least expect—except when it’s the most obvious one."
