# General Arthur "Ironhide" Montgomery

**Age**: 78
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: 4-Star General (U.S. Army), Combat Veteran.

## Profile
Arthur runs his wing of The Golden Years like a barracks. He wears his medals on his pajamas and conducts "inspections" of the corridors at 0500 hours. While he appears to be a rigid martinet, his discipline is a shield against the creeping fog of memory loss and the guilt of a "tactical error" that cost lives decades ago. He treats the residents as his unit, and he will do anything to ensure no one is "left behind."

## Quirks
- Refers to everyone by their "rank" (e.g., "Private Birthday Girl").
- Obsessed with tactical positioning; often draws "battle maps" of the dining hall on napkins.
- Carries a vintage whistle (non-functional) that he polishs when stressed.
- Salutes the portrait of the facility's founder every morning.
