# Lorenzo "Renzo" Romano

**Age**: 74
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: Hollywood Character Actor / Soap Opera Star (Best known for his role as "Dr. Drake Desperate" on *The Young and the Restless* for three years in the 80s).

## Profile
Lorenzo is silver-haired, self-absorbed, and believes he's still the heartthrob he was in 1978. But behind the dramatic poses and the constant name-dropping is a man terrified of the ultimate "curtain call." His vanity isn't just ego; it's a frantic attempt to remain "main cast" in a world that increasingly treats him as an extra. He lives as if a camera is always rolling because without an audience, he's not sure he exists at all.

## Quirks
- Quotes lines from his past roles to express real emotions (e.g., "As I told the Baroness in 'Shadows of Silk'... it's never too late for a martini").
- Checks his reflection in every shiny surface, but he's actually checking for signs of the illness he's secretly hiding.
- Hands out signed headshots from 1982 to the nursing staff instead of tips.
- Practices "The Smolder" with his walker, which he calls his "chariot of the gods."
- "The camera adds 10 pounds, so I stay thin" (he actually skips meals to maintain his 'leading man' frame).
