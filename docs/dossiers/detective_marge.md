# Detective Margaret "Marge" Cooper (Retired)

**Age**: 79
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: Police Detective

## Profile
Marge is sharp as a tack and immediately appoints herself lead investigator of the murder after the crappy one James hired is killed of. She carries a little notebook where she writes "observations" (mostly gossip). She thinks everyone else is an amateur sleuth and only SHE should be investigating.

## Quirks
- "In my day on the force..." (before every statement)
- Takes notes constantly
- Interrogates people instead of conversing
- Squints suspiciously at everyone
- Pulls out reading glasses with dramatic flair
