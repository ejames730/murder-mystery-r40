# Maria "La Chancla" Garcia

**Age**: 80
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Played by**: Angelica
**Past Occupation**: Former Professional Jai Alai Player (retired)

## Profile
Maria Garcia, better known throughout Shady Pines as "Ls Chancla," is the self-appointed enforcer of community etiquette. She is rarely seen without her signature weapon: a well-worn, pink rubber slipper that she can deploy with terrifying speed and pinpoint accuracy. While the staff tries to maintain "official" order, the residents know that if you skip the line at the early bird special or leave your TV too loud, a flying chancla is coming your way.

## Quirks
- Always holds one slipper in her hand, even while eating or playing bingo.
- Refers to herself in the third person as "La Chancla."
- Can hit a moving target from 30 feet away without looking.
- Mutters "dios mio" when she sees someone misbehaving.
- Has a collection of "trophy" items she's knocked out of people's hands (cell phones, excessive salt shakers, etc.).
