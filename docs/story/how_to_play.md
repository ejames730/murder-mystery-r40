# How to Play: The Shady Pines Mystery

Welcome to Shady Pines! tonight, you isn't just a guest; you are a detective, a suspect, or a witness. Here is how the evening will unfold.

---

## 🎭 Your Role

### 🌟 Primary Characters (Suspects)
If you have been assigned a **Primary Character**, you have a full dossier including a secret history, a personal motive, and a "clue" to share.
*   **Your Goal**: Deflect suspicion from yourself while trying to find the real killer.
*   **The Golden Rule**: You may lie about your **Secret**, but you MUST tell the truth about the **Clue** you possess if someone "extracts" it from you through better gossip or a bribe (like a bingo chip).

### 👥 Secondary Characters & Guests (Detectives)
If you are a **Staff Member** or a **Guest Detective**, you are the eyes and ears of the facility.
*   **Your Goal**: Talk to the residents, compare their stories, and use the **Detective's Log** to track who was where.
*   **The Golden Rule**: You are the only ones who can truly be "trusted." Use your influence to get the suspects to talk.

---

## 🕵️ The Game Flow

### Phase 1: The Social Hour
The party begins. Introduce yourself to as many residents as possible. Use the **[Character Manifest](dossiers/manifest.md)** to learn names and backgrounds. 
> [!TIP]
> Listen for people mentioning specific locations like the *Boiler Room* or the *Laundry Room*.

### Phase 2: The Investigation
A body is discovered! (Don't worry, it's probably just Mr. Henderson again... wait, no, this looks serious). At this point, the **Primary Characters** will receive their personal clues.
*   **Exchange Information**: You have to trade what you know to get what you need.
*   **Watch the BINGO Board**: The host will call out events; if they happen, it might trigger new evidence.

### Phase 3: The Final Reveal
Once the "Smoking Gun" evidence is found, the **Host** (Director James) will call everyone together. 
1.  All guests will submit their **Final Guess** on their tracking sheets.
2.  The Host will reveal the "Master Truth."
3.  The killer is apprehended (or sentenced to extra bingo duty).

---

## 📜 Three Rules for a Perfect Mystery
1.  **Stay in Character**: Use your quirks! If you're "Loud Rose," be loud. If you're the "Curmudgeon," weaponize your walker.
2.  **No Snooping in the Code**: Use the **[Guest Portal](index.html)** for your investigation. Looking at the "Secret" files in the GitHub repo is considered cheating!
3.  **The Goal is Fun**: It's not about being the world's best detective; it's about the drama, the gossip, and the ridiculous personalities of Shady Pines.
