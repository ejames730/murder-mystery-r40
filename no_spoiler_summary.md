# Non-Spoiler Summary: "Shady Pines Retirement Home"

This summary is designed to be shared with collaborators or potential players. It contains the theme, setting, and character descriptions **without any spoilers, secrets, or identity of the killer**.

---

## 🎭 Theme & Setting
**Shady Pines Retirement Community**
The year is 2026. Shady Pines is a "luxury" retirement community known for its early bird specials, competitive bingo, and residents who have lived very full (and sometimes mysterious) lives.

**The Hook**: A special 40th birthday party is being held for the "youngest admittee" (your wife). The residents have decided to show her how to *really* retire. But during the Championship Bingo round, tragedy strikes!

## 🛠️ Customization Guide
When reviewing these characters with your friend, consider:
1. **Personality Fit**: Which friend naturally embodies these archetypes?
2. **Quirk Adjustments**: Can a quirk be changed to a real-life habit of a guest to make it funnier?
3. **Occupation Tweaks**: Past occupations can be adjusted to match the guests' actual or former careers (in a "retired" context).
4. **Group Size**: If you have more than 12 guests, we can add more residents like "The Veteran," "The Artist," or "The Spinster."

## 🎲 Activity Highlights
- **Playable Bingo**: Not just a prop! You will actually play bingo rounds that reveal world information.
- **Natural Clues**: You won't just be handed cards; you have to talk to people and even "bribe" or persuade them.
- **Multiple Ways to Win**: Points are awarded for solving the murder, but also for best acting, best costume, and completing secret personal objectives.
