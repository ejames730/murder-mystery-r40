## The Discovery

## The Cause of Death (Quick Summary)
- **Official Cause**: Unknown (Initial appearance: Natural causes / heart attack).
- **The Truth**: They were poisoned. 
- **Murder Method Options** (Host can choose):
    1. **Poisoned prune juice** at the morning juice bar
    2. **Tampered blood pressure medication** from the medication room
    3. **Spiked early bird special** (the meatloaf was already suspicious)
    4. **Poisoned bingo dauber ink** (they licked it for luck)
    5. **Tainted Werther's Original candies** from the front desk bowl
    6. **Spiked denture cream** (diabolical!)

## The Key Evidence
- A missing vial from the medication room
- An unusual smell (almonds, prunes, or suspiciously minty breath)
- Witness testimonies that contradict each other (due to hearing problems and memory issues)
