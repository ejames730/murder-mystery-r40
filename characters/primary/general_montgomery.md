# General Arthur "Ironhide" Montgomery

**Age**: 78
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: 4-Star General (U.S. Army), Combat Veteran.

## Profile
Arthur runs his wing of The Golden Years like a barracks. He wears his medals on his pajamas and conducts "inspections" of the corridors at 0500 hours. While he appears to be a rigid martinet, his discipline is a shield against the creeping fog of memory loss and the guilt of a "tactical error" that cost lives decades ago. He treats the residents as his unit, and he will do anything to ensure no one is "left behind."

## Quirks
- Refers to everyone by their "rank" (e.g., "Private Birthday Girl").
- Obsessed with tactical positioning; often draws "battle maps" of the dining hall on napkins.
- Carries a vintage whistle (non-functional) that he polishs when stressed.
- Salutes the portrait of the facility's founder every morning.

## Secret
He is secretly paying the tuition for the grandson of a soldier who died under his command in 1968. He does this anonymously, and it’s why he’s always complaining about the "bureaucratic theft" (rising facility costs)—he’s actually running out of money to keep his promise.

## Relationships
- **Nurse Penny**: He respects her "medical efficiency" but suspects she's an enemy spy (or at least works for "Command" i.e., the administration).
- **Sophia**: They have a long-standing "treaty" over the shared reading room. He treats her with the respect of a fellow officer.
- **La Chancla**: He views her as an "unconventional warfare specialist" and stays out of her perimeter.

## Objective
To maintain "Operational Readiness" of the facility and protect his "troops" from the mismanagement of Dr. Sterling.

## Motive (if he's the killer)
Dr. Sterling threatened to kick Arthur out due to his "disruptive behavior," which would mean he could no longer keep an eye on his "unit." He saw Sterling as a corrupt officer who needed to be "relieved of duty" for the good of the men and women at Shady Pines.

## Clue (What he knows)
"I was performing a midnight perimeter sweep near the **boiler room** when I heard the vent rattling. Someone was in the maintenance shaft. They weren't fixing anything; they were *unscrewing* the safety valve. I also saw a pair of bright neon sneakers retreating toward the gym. Very poor camouflage choice."

## Hidden Evidence (If Arthur is the Killer)
*   **The Evidence**: A handwritten "After Action Report" detailing the "elimination of the target" for the safety of the unit.
*   **The Find**: Tucked inside his vintage army footlocker under a false bottom.
*   **The Link**: The report uses the same specific tactical terminology Arthur uses in conversation, and it's written on the back of a Shady Pines menu.
