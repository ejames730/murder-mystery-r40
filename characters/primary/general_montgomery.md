# General "Ironhide" Montgomery

**Age**: 78
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: 4-Star General (U.S. Army)

## Profile
The General runs his wing of The Golden Years like a barracks. He wears his medals on his pajamas and conducts "inspection" of the corridors at 0500 hours.

## Quirk
Refers to everyone by their "rank" (e.g., "Private Birthday Girl"). He is obsessed with tactical positioning and often draws "battle maps" of the dining hall on napkins.

## Motive
Dr. Sterling recently issued a "Demilitarization Order," confiscating the General's collection of antique (disabled) sidearms and banning his 0500 inspections. The General views this as a direct assault on his sovereignty.

## Clue (What he knows)
"I saw that 'Medic' (Nurse Penny) sneaking into the Director's office with a vial that definitely wasn't Vitamin C. It was a tactical extraction, I tell you!"
