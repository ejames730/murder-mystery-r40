# Nurse Penelope "Penny" Pincher

**Age**: 34
**Role**: Primary Staff / Suspect
**Current Occupation**: Head Nurse at The Golden Years.

## Profile
Penny is the backbone of the facility. She's seen it all, cleaned it all, and survived it all. She's efficiency personified, often seen speed-walking through the halls with a clipboard.

## Quirk
She has a "clinical" way of describing everything, even social interactions (e.g., "The patient is exhibiting symptoms of extreme birthday-itis"). She constantly checks people's pulses while talking to them.

## Motive
Dr. Sterling denied her request for a weekend off—her first in two years. He also threatened to "review her performance" after she spoke up about the lack of supplies. Penny knows she's the only one keeping the place running and feels she deserves his job.

## Clue (What she knows)
"I noticed a 10ml vial of 'The Good Stuff' (a potent sedative) was missing from the locked cabinet. The log says it was Dr. Sterling himself who took it, but the signature looks... hurried. Or forged."
