# Nurse Penelope "Penny" Pincher

**Age**: 34
**Role**: Primary Staff / Suspect
**Current Occupation**: Head Nurse at The Golden Years.

## Profile
Penny is the backbone of the facility. She's seen it all, cleaned it all, and survived it all. She's efficiency personified, often seen speed-walking through the halls with a clipboard.

## Quirk
She has a "clinical" way of describing everything, even social interactions (e.g., "The patient is exhibiting symptoms of extreme birthday-itis"). She constantly checks people's pulses while talking to them.

## Motive
Dr. Sterling denied her request for a weekend off—her first in two years. He also threatened to "review her performance" after she spoke up about the lack of supplies. Penny knows she's the only one keeping the place running and feels she deserves his job.

## Clue (What she knows)
"I noticed the **emergency panic button** in the **Director's office** had been tampered with. The wires were cut with surgical precision—not the kind of hack-job you'd expect from a clumsy resident. And someone has been using 'the backup' medical fridge to store... well, it wasn't medicine. It looked like a very expensive bottle of vintage wine that definitely isn't allowed on the facility grounds."

## Hidden Evidence (If Penny is the Killer)
*   **The Evidence**: A "Code Blue" kit that has been tampered with; the adrenaline has been replaced with a high-dose paralyzing agent.
*   **The Find**: Inside her personal locker, hidden in a double-bottomed medical bag.
*   **The Link**: The kit has a timestamp of being checked out at 0200 hours last night—a time when Penny claims she was in the cafeteria.
