# "The Lothario" - Lorenzo "Renzo" Romano

**Age**: 74
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: Hollywood Character Actor / Soap Opera Star

## Profile
Lorenzo is silver-haired, self-absorbed, and believes he's still the heartthrob he was in 1978. He's sleeping with (or trying to sleep with) multiple residents and staff members. He keeps headshots from his acting days and hands them out like business cards.

## Quirks
- Quotes lines from his soap opera "Days of Our Retirement"
- Checks his reflection in every shiny surface
- Name-drops celebrities he "totally worked with"
- Practices dramatic poses with his walker
- "The camera adds 10 pounds, so I stay thin"

## Secret
He's juggling romantic relationships with at least 4 different people at Shady Pines. They're all starting to discover each other.

## Objective
Maintain all his relationships simultaneously OR seduce the birthday girl to prove he's "still got it."

## Motive (if he's the killer)
The victim was going to reveal his multiple affairs at bingo night, which would end his romantic empire.

## Clue (What he knows)
"Darling, I saw something absolutely dramatic! Someone was in the medication room, looking very suspicious. I was going to investigate, but I had to get ready for my close-up at bingo. A star must always be camera-ready, you understand."
