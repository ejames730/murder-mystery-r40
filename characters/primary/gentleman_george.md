# "Gentleman" George Sterling

**Age**: 81
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: Investment Banker

## Profile
George is distinguished, charming, and always dressed impeccably (or as impeccably as one can be in velour tracksuits). He's known as "The Gentleman" for his old-fashioned manners and smooth talking. But beneath the surface, he's hiding a scandalous past that could ruin his carefully cultivated image.

## Quirks
- Kisses ladies' hands (even though most find it strange)
- Quotes poetry at inappropriate moments
- Always carries a handkerchief and offers it dramatically
- Refers to everyone as "my dear boy" or "my dear girl"
- Forgets mid-conversation that he already told that story

## Secret
He's not actually wealthy—he lost everything in a bad investment 20 years ago. He's been living off the generosity of romantic admirers ever since. He's currently "courting" three different wealthy widows for their money.

## Objective
Secure a marriage proposal from the richest widow before anyone discovers he's broke.

## Motive (if he's the killer)
The victim found out about his financial situation and threatened to expose him to his wealthy "prospects."

## Clue (What he knows)
"My dear, I witnessed the most ungentlemanly behavior earlier. Someone was rifling through the medication cabinet like a common burglar. I would have intervened, but my sciatica was acting up."
