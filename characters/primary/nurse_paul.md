# Nurse Paul Pincher

**Age**: 34
**Role**: Primary Staff / Suspect
**Played by**: [Friend]
**Current Occupation**: Head Nurse at Shady Pines.

## Profile
Paul is the backbone of the facility. He's seen it all, cleaned it all, and survived it all. He's efficiency personified, often seen speed-walking through the halls with a clipboard. He takes his job with deadly seriousness and has zero patience for anyone who "disrupts the flow" of the medical ward.

## Quirk
He has a "clinical" way of describing everything, even social interactions (e.g., "The patient is exhibiting symptoms of extreme birthday-itis"). He constantly checks people's pulses while talking to them, often doing it mid-sentence.

## Motive
The victim denied Paul's request for a weekend off—his first in two years. They also threatened to "review his performance" after he spoke up about the lack of supplies. Paul knows he's the only one keeping the place running and felt that the victim was an incompetent obstacle that needed to be... removed.

## Clue (What she knows)
"I noticed the **emergency panic button** in the **Director's office** had been tampered with. The wires were cut with surgical precision—not the kind of hack-job you'd expect from a clumsy resident. And someone has been using 'the backup' medical fridge to store... well, it wasn't medicine. It looked like a very expensive bottle of vintage wine that definitely isn't allowed on the facility grounds."

## Hidden Evidence (If Penny is the Killer)
*   **The Evidence**: A "Code Blue" kit that has been tampered with; the adrenaline has been replaced with a high-dose paralyzing agent.
*   **The Find**: Inside her personal locker, hidden in a double-bottomed medical bag.
*   **The Link**: The kit has a timestamp of being checked out at 0200 hours last night—a time when Penny claims she was in the cafeteria.
