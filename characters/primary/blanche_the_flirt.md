# Blanche "The Flirt" Devereaux

**Age**: 76
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: Real Estate Agent (specialized in "charming" difficult clients)

## Profile
Blanche is glamorous, flirtatious, and "forgets" her glasses so she has to lean in VERY close to the male residents when talking. She wears perfume that could knock out a bingo caller at 50 paces. What nobody knows is that she's actually owns a substantial share of the retirement home business, planted in Shady Pines undercover to investigate suspected fraud.

## Quirks
- Hits on every male resident regardless of age or consciousness level
- "Oh my stars!" (her favorite expression)
- Constantly reapplying lipstick
- "Forgets" her glasses to get closer to people
- Tells exaggerated stories about her many suitors

## Secret
She's undercover. Her "daughter who never visits" is actually her handler. She's investigating financial irregularities and medication theft at Shady Pines.

## Objective
Sabotage someone's reputation to make them look guilty OR expose the illegal activity she's discovered (the gambling ring, the medication theft, etc.).

## Motive (if she's the killer)
The victim discovered she was undercover and threatened to blow her investigation. She had to silence them.

## Clue (What she knows)
"Well sugar, I was just freshening up my makeup in the powder room when I heard someone arguing about pills. Not the fun kind, the boring medicine kind. They were saying something about 'missing inventory' and 'you're going to get us both caught.' Very suspicious, don't you think?"
