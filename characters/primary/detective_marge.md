# Detective Margaret "Marge" Cooper (Retired)

**Age**: 79
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: Police Detective

## Profile
Marge is sharp as a tack and immediately appoints herself lead investigator of the murder. She carries a little notebook where she writes "observations" (mostly gossip). She thinks everyone else is an amateur sleuth and only SHE should be investigating.

## Quirks
- "In my day on the force..." (before every statement)
- Takes notes constantly
- Interrogates people instead of conversing
- Squints suspiciously at everyone
- Pulls out reading glasses with dramatic flair

## Secret
She was actually asked to retire early due to "creative interpretations" of evidence. She's not as good a detective as she thinks she is.

## Objective
Solve the murder FIRST to prove she's still got it, even if it means planting evidence or leading people to the wrong conclusion.

## Motive (if she's the killer)
The victim knew about her forced retirement and was going to tell everyone, destroying her reputation as Shady Pines' resident detective.

## Clue (What she knows)
"According to my notes, I observed suspicious activity at 18:47 hours near the medication room. The suspect was approximately 5'6" or maybe 6'2"—my bifocals were foggy. But I'll crack this case, mark my words!"
