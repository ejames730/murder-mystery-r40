# The Birthday Girl - [Wife's Name]

**Age**: 70
**Role**: Primary Resident / The New One
**Past Occupation**: Teacher

## Profile
The Birthday Girl is the "new resident" being initiated into Shady Pines as a hilarious 70th birthday joke. She's the youngest person there and the residents treat her like a naive spring chicken who needs to "learn the ropes." She can play this either genuinely confused or totally embracing the bit.

## Quirks (To Develop During Party)
- Genuinely shocked by how early the early bird special is
- Asking what things cost "back in your day"
- Pretending to need help with basic tasks
- OR fully committing to being a grumpy new resident who complains about everything

## Secret
She's actually having the time of her life and/or she's really worried about turning 70.

## Objective
Survive her 70th birthday party / Figure out whodunit to prove she's still sharp (even at the ancient age of 70) / Make her husband pay for this party idea.

## Special Role
As the "newest resident," she has fresh eyes on everyone. She can be the one to ultimately solve the murder or make the final accusation.

## Clue (What she knows)
"I don't know what's normal around here, but I saw someone acting REALLY weird near [location]. Then again, everyone here seems to act weird, so maybe that IS normal?"
