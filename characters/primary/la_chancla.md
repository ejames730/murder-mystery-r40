# Maria "La Chancla" Garcia

**Age**: 80
**Role**: Primary Resident / Suspect
**Past Occupation**: Former Professional Jai Alai Player (retired)

## Profile
Maria Garcia, better known throughout Shady Pines as "Ls Chancla," is the self-appointed enforcer of community etiquette. She is rarely seen without her signature weapon: a well-worn, pink rubber slipper that she can deploy with terrifying speed and pinpoint accuracy. While the staff tries to maintain "official" order, the residents know that if you skip the line at the early bird special or leave your TV too loud, a flying chancla is coming your way.

## Quirks
- Always holds one slipper in her hand, even while eating or playing bingo.
- Refers to herself in the third person as "La Chancla."
- Can hit a moving target from 30 feet away without looking.
- Mutters "dios mio" when she sees someone misbehaving.
- Has a collection of "trophy" items she's knocked out of people's hands (cell phones, excessive salt shakers, etc.).

## Secret
She was actually a world-champion Jai Alai player in her youth (under an alias), which explains her superhuman flick of the wrist and deadly aim. She's also the secret muscle for Rose's illegal gambling ring, she ensures everyone pays their debts on time.

## Objective
To ensure that "order and respect" are maintained at Shady Pines, and to collect a 500-dollar debt from the victim that they tried to "forget."

## Motive (if she's the killer)
The victim not only refused to pay their gambling debt but also had the audacity to call her "just a little old lady." She decided to show them just how much a "little old lady" can do with a perfectly aimed strike to a vital point.

## Clue (What she knows)
"La Chancla sees all. I was in the **courtyard** practicing my backhand against the wall when I saw someone in the **pharmacy office**. They weren't taking pills; they were taking a file. A big, blue folder. They tucked it into their waistband and practically ran toward the cafeteria. I was going to throw my slipper to teach them a lesson about speed limits, but they were already gone. But I saw their shoes—expensive, shiny, and completely wrong for floor-waxing night."

## Hidden Evidence (If Maria is the Killer)
*   **The Evidence**: A "Special Edition" heavy-weighted rubber slipper that has been modified with a lead core.
*   **The Find**: Hidden in a box labeled "Jai Alai Equipment" under her bed.
*   **The Link**: The slipper matches the exact circular indentation found on the victim's temple, and it has a faint trace of the victim's expensive hair pomade on the toe.
