# Diane - Front Desk Administrator

**Age**: 52
**Role**: NPC / Staff Member
**Occupation**: Front Desk Administrator at Shady Pines

## Profile
Diane is OBSESSED with the sign-in sheet. She mentions it constantly. She's bored out of her mind at the front desk and LOVES drama—she'll gossip with anyone who stops by. She constantly reminisces about "when she was younger" (which was apparently much more exciting).

## Quirks
- "Did you sign in? Everyone must sign in!"
- "Back when I was your age..." (to 80-year-olds)
- Chewing gum loudly
- Has every resident's mail memorized
- "It's policy!" (even when it's not)
- Starts gossip just to have something to talk about

## What She Knows (Clue Distribution)
Diane sits at the front desk and tracks:
- Who came and went (via the sacred sign-in sheet)
- Who had visitors
- What packages/deliveries arrived
- Who was arguing in the lobby
- Who left the building and when they returned
- Someone signed out but never signed back in

## Character Trait
Bored. Loves drama. Somewhat unreliable memory mixed with very accurate records. Lives vicariously through residents' lives.

## How to Interact
She'll share her clues if you:
- Sign the sign-in sheet properly
- Listen to her stories about "the good old days"
- Ask about packages or mail
- Bring her gossip in exchange
