# Frank - Handyman

**Age**: 67
**Role**: NPC / Staff Member
**Occupation**: Handyman at Shady Pines

## Profile
Frank mumbles constantly, is genuinely hard of hearing, and says "Eh?" after every sentence. He moves at the pace of cold molasses. But here's the secret: Frank notices EVERYTHING. He's just not telling anyone unless they ask directly multiple times.

## Quirks
- Mumbles incoherently
- "Eh? What's that now?"
- Moves incredibly slowly with his toolbox
- Adjusts his hearing aid constantly (it may or may not be working)
- Pretends not to understand questions
- When he finally talks, he reveals he knew EVERYTHING

## What He Knows (Clue Distribution)
Frank is everywhere fixing things and knows:
- Which residents sneak into restricted areas
- Arguments he overheard through walls/vents while fixing things
- Who was where at what time (he notices everything)
- Secret passages or maintenance areas people don't know about
- Someone hiding something in the mechanical room

## Character Trait
Silent observer. Notices everything, tells no one. Must be asked multiple times before he shares information. Actually quite wise.

## How to Interact
He'll share his clues if you:
- Ask him at least 3 times (he'll say "Eh?" the first two)
- Speak directly into his hearing aid
- Help him carry his toolbox
- Wait patiently while he ruminates
