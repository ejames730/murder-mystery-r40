# Frank "The Ear" Morrison - Handyman

**Age**: 67
**Role**: NPC / Staff Member
**Occupation**: Handyman at Shady Pines (Former Investigative Journalist).

## Profile
Frank mumbles constantly, is genuinely hard of hearing, and says "Eh?" after every sentence. He moves at the pace of cold molasses. But Frank isn't just a slow-moving repairman; he's a man who spent forty years digging up secrets for the *Chicago Tribune*. He "retired" to Shady Pines to live a quiet life after one too many threats from the people he exposed. He views the residents not as seniors, but as subjects in a lifelong study of human nature.

## Quirks
- Mumbles incoherently (actually he's often reciting facts about the person he's looking at).
- "Eh? What's that now?" (Used to buy time while he processes information).
- Moves incredibly slowly with his toolbox (which contains a high-end digital recorder hidden among the wrenches).
- Adjusts his hearing aid constantly (it’s actually a sophisticated listening device he modified himself).
- Pretends not to understand questions to see if people will reveal more than they intended.
- Whistles "The Entertainer" when he's found a particularly juicy piece of gossip.

## Secret
Frank isn't just hearing things; he's documenting them. He has a secret "Dossier" on every resident and staff member hidden in the wall behind the boiler room. He knows who’s stealing medication, who’s faking their illnesses, and who’s sleeping with whom. He’s not a blackmailer, though—he’s a collector of truth.

## Relationships
- **Dr. Sterling**: Frank dislikes the Director's "corporate efficiency" and believes Sterling is cutting corners on safety. Frank has been "forgetting" to fix the Director's office lock properly for weeks.
- **La Chancla**: They have a mutual respect. She provides the "muscle," and Frank provides the "intelligence." They sometimes share coffee at 3 AM in the maintenance closet.
- **Nurse Penny**: He suspects she's onto him. He keeps his "Eh?" game extra strong when she's around.

## What He Knows (Clue Distribution)
"Eh? What’s that? The murder? Right. I was fixing a leak in the **attic** and I saw someone messing with the ventilation fan. They weren't cleaning it; they were dripping something into the intake. Smelled like a laboratory. I also saw someone drop a distinctive **purple knitting needle** into the air duct. I haven't seen poor Mrs. Higgins use her purple ones in days."

## Hidden Evidence (If Frank is the Killer)
*   **The Evidence**: A "Master Key" that clearly wasn't issued by the facility, and a folder of high-resolution photos of the victim in compromising positions.
*   **The Find**: In a hollowed-out "Home Repair" manual in the maintenance closet.
*   **The Link**: The photos were taken from an angle only accessible from the maintenance crawlspace, and the key has Frank's unique "muffled" keychain on it.


## Character Trait
The "Invisible Man." People talk in front of him as if he's part of the furniture. He is actually the most informed person in the building.

## How to Interact
He'll share his clues if you:
- Ask him at least 3 times (the "Triple-Eh" rule).
- Mention a "headline" or "breaking news"—it triggers his old journalist instincts.
- Bring him a "real" cup of coffee (not the watered-down facility stuff).
- Show him a piece of evidence; he can't resist analyzing it.
