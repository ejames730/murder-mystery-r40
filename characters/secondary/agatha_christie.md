# Agatha "The Plot" Christie

**Age**: 82
**Role**: NPC (Secondary Resident)
**Past Occupation**: Mystery Novelist (Author of *The Denture of Destiny*).

## Profile
Agatha doesn't say much, but her eyes are constantly scanning the room for "plot points." She views everyone as a character in a first-draft manuscript. She’s the facility’s resident expert on "how it’s always the person you least expect—except when it’s the most obvious one."

## Clue (The "Hook")
"I was sitting in the **lobby** at 3 AM—inspiration usually strikes then—and I saw the **front desk clerk** handing a guest key to someone who wasn't a guest. They looked very official, almost like a doctor, but they were wearing a pair of **neon-green athletic shoes**. I remember thinking, 'That’s a classic red herring if I ever saw one!'"

## Natural Interaction
She can be found sitting near the "acting spurt" locations, muttering to herself about "pacing" and "motives." She’ll share her clue with anyone who helps her find her "missing" typewriter ribbon (it’s usually on her head).
