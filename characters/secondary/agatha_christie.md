# Agatha "The Ear" Christie

**Age**: 82
**Role**: NPC (Secondary Resident)
**Past Occupation**: Telephone Operator.

## Profile
Agatha doesn't say much, but her ears are always twitching. She's the facility's unofficial news agency.

## Clue (The "Hook")
"Oh, I shouldn't say, but... I saw the Groundskeeper hiding something in the flowerbeds right after the lights flickered. Looked like a small glass bottle."

## Natural Interaction
She can be found sitting near the "acting spurt" locations, whispering her clue to anyone who offers her a piece of birthday cake.
