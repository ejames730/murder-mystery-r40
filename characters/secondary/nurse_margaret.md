# Nurse Margaret

**Age**: 45
**Role**: NPC / Staff Member
**Occupation**: Head Nurse at Shady Pines

## Profile
Margaret speaks VERY SLOWLY and LOUDLY to all residents, even though most of them can hear perfectly fine. She's overprotective, treats all the residents like children, and loves gossip with the other staff members.

## Quirks
- "CAN. YOU. HEAR. ME. SWEETIE?" (to residents with perfect hearing)
- Makes exaggerated hand gestures while talking slowly
- Calls everyone "sweetie," "honey," or "dear"
- Constantly checking people's blood pressure for no reason
- Gossips constantly but whispers "I shouldn't tell you this, but..."

## What She Knows (Clue Distribution)
Margaret has access to the medication room and knows:
- Who has been taking extra medications
- Who was seen near the medication room
- Who came to her with "suspicious ailments"
- Someone stole from the donation box last week
- Two residents were arguing about pills

## Character Trait
Overprotective to the point of annoyance. Gossipy. Means well but treats everyone like they're 5 years old.

## How to Interact
She'll share her clues if you:
- Let her check your blood pressure
- Listen to her complain about residents
- Pretend you can't hear so she can "help" you
