# Mr. James Tasker - Retirement Home Director

**Age**: 62
**Role**: Host / Administrator / Potential Suspect
**Personality**: Stressed, Frugal, and Secretly Soft-Hearted.

## Profile
As the Director of Shady Pines, you are the face of an institution that is slowly sinking. Your reputation for being "frugal"—buying generic denture cream and lowering the heat—isn't born of greed, but of a desperate, late-night battle with a spreadsheet that refuses to balance. You’re trying to save Shady Pines from being bought out by "The Silver Horizon," a corporate chain that would triple the residents' rent. You view the residents as a collection of difficult, expensive, and deeply beloved grandparents you are failing to protect.

## Your Background
You came to Shady Pines expecting a quiet end-of-career post. Instead, you found a facility crumbling under decades of mismanagement. You’ve been working sixteen-hour days, often doing the laundry yourself to save on staffing costs. You hired a "budget" professional investigator because you were desperate to find out who was sabotaging the facility’s equipment, but as usual, you tried to save money and ended up with a headache.

## Your Quirks
- **Frugal to a Fault**: You constantly mention costs ("Do you know the price of wholesale Jell-O? It's scandalous!"), but it’s a nervous tic.
- **The "Director" Voice**: You use a booming, authoritative voice to hide the fact that your hands are shaking.
- **Micro-Manager**: You obsess over things like missing TV remotes because they are the only things you feel you *can* control.
- **Hidden Sweets**: You carry a stash of high-quality chocolates to give to residents when they’re upset, but you always claim they were "donated."

## Your Secret (The "Budget" Investigator)
You hired Detective "Slippery" Sam (the secondary victim) to investigate "sabotage" at the facility. However, you realized too late that Sam was a fraud who was actually stealing from the residents himself. When you confronted him, he turned the tables and threatened to expose the "creative accounting" you’ve been doing to keep the lights on—accounting that looks a lot like embezzlement to an outsider.

## Relationships
- **General Montgomery**: You deeply respect his sense of duty and wish you could tell him why the "battle maps" (facility repairs) aren't happening.
- **Frank**: You suspect Frank knows everything about your financial situation. You’re terrified of him, yet he’s the only one you trust to keep the boiler running.
- **Rose**: You feel a deep sense of guilt about her birthday party because you had to "vandalize" the activities budget to pay for the cake.

## Your Objective
1. **Save the Home**: Find a way to balance the books without selling out to the corporate sharks.
2. **Handle the "Detective"**: Get the "evidence" he has against you and get him out of the building.
3. **Solve the Murder**: Find the killer quickly to prevent a scandal that would give "The Silver Horizon" the leverage they need to take over.

## Motive (If you are the killer)
Slippery Sam was going to report your "creative accounting" to the board tomorrow. This wouldn't just cost you your job; it would lead to the immediate closure of Shady Pines and the displacement of every resident you've grown to love. You decided that one dead crook was a small price to pay to keep thirty seniors in their homes.

## Clue (What you know)
"Listen, I was in the **basement storage** checking the inventory of generic toilet paper (it’s a gold mine down there) when I heard someone crying. I peeked through the boxes and saw someone throwing a heavy, jade cat statue into the trash incinerator. It was covered in something dark. I would have stopped them, but the incinerator costs fifty cents a minute to run and I didn't want to waste the fuel!"

## Hidden Evidence (If James is the Killer)
*   **The Evidence**: A ledger showing that the "Social Fund" is completely empty, and the final entry is a large payment to a "Private Debtor" made tonight.
*   **The Find**: Locked in the "Budget Savings" safe in his office (the code is 0000).
*   **The Link**: The payment amount exactly matches the blackmail demand found in the victim's pocket, and the "Debtor" name is a known alias of the victim.
