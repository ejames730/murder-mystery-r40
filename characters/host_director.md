# Mr./Ms. [Your Name] - Retirement Home Director

**Age**: [Your Choice]
**Role**: Host / Administrator / Potential Suspect
**Personality**: Stressed, Cheap, and Overwhelmed

## Profile
As the Director of Shady Pines, you are in charge of everything, meaning you are blamed for everything. You have a reputation for being... let's say "frugal." You buy the generic brand of denture cream, you turned the thermostat down to 62 during the winter, and you recently hired a "budget" professional investigator because you were suspicious of a local resident.

## Your Background
You were hired to bring "efficiency" (budget cuts) to Shady Pines. You think the residents are adorable but expensive. You've been trying to solve a minor mystery at the facility yourself for weeks, but after failing miserably, you hired a professional—then immediately regretted it because of his hourly rate.

## Your Quirks
- **Frugal to a Fault**: You constantly mention how much things cost. "Do you know how much electricity those oxygen tanks use?"
- **The Juggling Act**: You are constantly trying to look professional while suppressing a panic attack.
- **The "Director" Voice**: You talk like you're giving a press release even when you're just ordering pizza.
- **Sweating the Small Stuff**: You care more about the missing TV remote than the actual physical safety of the residents.

## Your Secret (The "Budget" Investigator)
You hired Detective "Slippery" Sam (the secondary victim) to investigate a resident who you suspected of stealing from the facility’s petty cash. However, you hired him from a "discount detective" app, and he was actually a complete fraud. Even worse, you owe him several weeks' worth of back pay, and he was threatening to expose your questionable management of the community's funds if you didn't pay up tonight.

## Your Objective
1. **Host the Party**: Keep the residents happy and the Birthday Girl celebrated.
2. **Handle the "Detective"**: Try to get him to lower his fee (or just avoid him).
3. **Solve the Murder**: Find out who killed the detective—but make sure it doesn't look like you did it for the money.

## Motive (If you are the killer)
The detective found out you were skimming from the community’s social fund to pay for your wife’s 40th birthday extravaganza. He tried to blackmail you, and you decided that a "unfortunate accident" during Bingo was cheaper than paying him off.

## Clue (What you know)
"Listen, I didn't see anything. I was too busy worrying about the price of those bingo daubers. Did you see the quality? They leak! They're cheap! ...Wait, that's beside the point. I *did* see someone talking to Sam near the shuffleboard court earlier. They looked very intense. Or maybe they were just squinting. My glasses are generic and they're terrible."
