## 🗺️ Shady Pines Retirement Community Map

### Image Generation Prompt

```
Create a top-down floor plan / map of "Shady Pines Retirement Community" in a vintage, slightly cartoonish style with a beige/cream color palette. The map should include these labeled locations:

MAIN BUILDING:
- Bingo Hall / Community Room (large central space)
- Dining Hall (with tables marked "Early Bird Special Zone")
- TV Lounge (with "Remote Last Seen Here" marker)
- Medication Room (with a red cross symbol)
- Nurse's Station (Margaret's desk)
- Front Desk (Diane's domain, with sign-in sheet icon)
- Activity Room (with rejected crafts)
- Director's Office (marked "Restricted")

OUTDOOR AREAS:
- Shuffleboard Court (with lines marked)
- Garden / Flowerbeds (suspicious hiding spots)

HALLWAYS:
- East Wing Resident Rooms
- West Wing Resident Rooms

Style notes: Vintage nursing home aesthetic, warm colors, slightly humorous labels, small icons for each location (walker icon, bingo dauber, medication bottle, etc.). Include a compass rose and "Shady Pines Est. 1982" in corner.
```

### Alternative Text-Based Map Prompt for Document Generation

```
Create a simple, clean floor plan diagram for "Shady Pines Retirement Community" using ASCII art or simple shapes. Label all key locations: Bingo Hall (center), Dining Hall, TV Lounge, Medication Room, Nurse's Station, Front Desk, Activity Room, Director's Office, Shuffleboard Court (outside), Garden, East Wing (resident rooms), West Wing (resident rooms). Use a vintage retirement home aesthetic with clear labels and a legend.
```

## 📄 Character Bio Handout Sheets (Primary Characters)

### Template Prompt (Repeat for each character)

```
Create a character bio handout sheet for a murder mystery party with a retirement home theme. The handout should be formatted as a single-page document with the following sections:

CHARACTER: [Character Name and Nickname]
AGE: [Age]
ROLE: Resident / Staff
PAST OCCUPATION: [Occupation]

YOUR PROFILE:
[2-3 sentences describing your personality and background]

YOUR QUIRKS:
• [Quirk 1]
• [Quirk 2]
• [Quirk 3]

YOUR SECRET:
[One major secret only this character knows]

YOUR OBJECTIVES (complete for bonus points!):
□ [Objective 1] - X points
□ [Objective 2] - X points
□ [Objective 3] - X points

WHAT YOU KNOW (Your Clue):
[The clue this character can share with others]

RELATIONSHIPS:
• [Relationship to Character A]
• [Relationship to Character B]

═══════════════════════════════
STARTING TOKENS: 10
TIPS FOR PLAYING THIS CHARACTER:
[2-3 sentences on how to embody this role]

Style: Vintage retirement home aesthetic, warm beige/cream colors, slightly aged paper texture, readable fonts (minimum 12pt), include small icon of character archetype (walker, pearls, hearing aid, etc.)
```

### Example: Dorothy's Bio Sheet Prompt

```
Create a character bio handout sheet for "Dorothy 'The Widow' Morrison" for a retirement home murder mystery:

CHARACTER: Dorothy "The Widow" Morrison
AGE: 78
ROLE: Resident
PAST OCCUPATION: High School Principal

YOUR PROFILE:
You're elegant, sharp-witted, and mention your "late husband Harold" constantly. Harold was apparently an expert on everything. What you don't mention is that you're currently dating TWO residents: Bernie and Sal. They're starting to figure it out.

YOUR QUIRKS:
• Start advice with "Harold always said..."
• Constantly adjust your pearls when nervous
• Have different stories about how Harold died
• Carry a framed photo of "Harold" (it's a stock photo)

YOUR SECRET:
You're juggling two relationships and trying to keep them from discovering each other.

YOUR OBJECTIVES:
□ Keep both Bernie AND Sal from finding out about each other (2 points)
□ Frame one of them for the murder if they discover your secret (3 points)
□ Mention Harold at least 20 times during the party (1 point)

WHAT YOU KNOW:
"I saw someone sneaking into the medication room right before bingo. At first I thought it was Harold's ghost, but then I remembered Harold would never wear those shoes."

RELATIONSHIPS:
• Secretly dating Bernie (the kind one with the good hearing aid)
• Also secretly dating Sal (the suave former salesman)
• Respects Detective Marge but finds her annoying
• Tolerates Rose's loudness

STARTING TOKENS: 10

TIPS FOR PLAYING THIS CHARACTER:
Channel your inner matriarch. Be dignified but dramatic. Harold is your excuse for everything. When caught in a lie, invoke Harold. Adjust your pearls frequently when stressed!

Style: Elegant vintage design, pearl border decoration, warm cream color, readable serif font
```

## 📝 Player Tracking Sheet

### Prompt for Tracking Sheet Design

```
Create a player tracking sheet for a murder mystery dinner party, suitable for printing on standard 8.5x11" paper. The sheet should have a vintage retirement home aesthetic with these sections:

HEADER:
- "SHADY PINES INVESTIGATION LOG"
- Player Name field
- Character Name field
- Starting Tokens: 10

SECTION 1: CLUES DISCOVERED (0.5 pts each)
- 12 checkbox lines for writing discovered clues
- Formula at bottom: ___ clues × 0.5 = ___ pts

SECTION 2: SUSPECTS & NOTES
- Table with 3 columns: Name | Motive | Suspicious Behavior
- 8-10 rows for tracking all characters

SECTION 3: CHARACTER OBJECTIVES (checked off when complete)
- 3 checkbox lines (to be filled in from character sheet)
- Point values next to each
- Total objectives points: ___

SECTION 4: LOCATION TRACKER
- Grid of locations visited with checkboxes:
  □ Bingo Hall  □ Medication Room  □ Dining Hall
  □ TV Lounge   □ Nurse's Station  □ Front Desk
  □ Activity Room  □ Shuffleboard Court

SECTION 5: TOKEN TRACKING
- Starting: 10 tokens
- Earned: ___ tokens (from what: _________)
- Spent: ___ tokens (on what: _________)
- Remaining: ___ tokens ÷ 5 = ___ pts

SECTION 6: FINAL ACCUSATION
- Who is the killer? ___________
- Murder method? ___________
- Supporting evidence:
  1. _________________
  2. _________________

SECTION 7: SCORING SUMMARY
- Clues: ___ pts
- Objectives: ___ pts
- Tokens: ___ pts
- Bonuses: ___ pts
- TOTAL: ___ pts

Style: Vintage paper texture, aged beige/cream, decorative borders, clear sections, readable 11-12pt font, small icons for each section (magnifying glass, notepad, poker chip, etc.)
```

## 🎫 Shady Pines "Bucks" (Token Currency)

```

- Vintage retirement home imagery: walkers, bingo daubers, playing cards
```

## 🎨 Using These Prompts

### For Image Generation AI:
1. Copy the specific prompt
2. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion
3. Adjust style parameters as needed
4. Generate multiple variations
5. Select best results for printing

### For Document/Template Generation:
1. Use prompts with ChatGPT, Claude, or Canva AI
2. Request editable formats (Word, Google Docs, PDF)
3. Customize with party-specific details
4. Print in color for best effect

### Quick Tips:
- **Paper**: Use cream/beige cardstock for vintage feel
- **Font**: Use readable serif fonts (minimum 12pt)
- **Icons**: Add small visual elements for personality
- **Aging**: Crumple slightly and smooth out for worn look
