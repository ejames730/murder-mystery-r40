# 🕵️‍♂️ Murder at Shady Pines: Character Selection & Interest Sheet

Hello future resident (or staff member) of **Shady Pines Retirement Community!**

We are hosting a murder mystery party for [Wife's Name]'s 40th birthday, and we need YOU to help bring the chaos to life. Please take a look at the roles below and let us know what kind of character you'd like to play and how much "work" you want to do.

---

## 🎭 Choose Your Level of Involvement

**1. The Star Suspect (High Involvement)**
*   **What it means**: You are a primary resident. You have a deep backstory, a spicy secret, and a potential motive for murder. You'll need to talk to everyone, defend yourself, and maybe even lie through your teeth.
*   **Best for**: People who love acting, improv, or being the center of attention.

**2. The Observant Neighbor (Medium Involvement)**
*   **What it means**: You are a resident or a regular visitor. You have fewer secrets to hide but plenty of "tea" to spill. You'll have specific clues about other people that you can share... for a price.
*   **Best for**: People who want to participate and solve the mystery without the pressure of being the killer.

**3. The Staff Member (Atmospheric Involvement)**
*   **What it means**: You keep the community running. You're there to "work" (and annoy the residents). You carry the most reliable clues and help move the story along.
*   **Best for**: People who want a clear "job" to do, like to interact in short bursts, or just want to watch the drama unfold.

## 👥 Meet the Residents of Shady Pines

*   **Dorothy (The Widow)**: Elegant, sharp-witted, and mentions her late husband Harold in every sentence.
*   **Rose (The Gossip)**: Sweet, loud, and can't remember anyone's name. She hears everything (or thinks she does).
*   **Blanche (The Flirt)**: A Southern belle who "forgets" her glasses to get closer to the handsome gentlemen.
*   **Sophia (The Curmudgeon)**: Tiny, fierce, and weaponizes her walker. Known for falling asleep mid-insult.
*   **LA chancla (The Matriarch)**: The self-appointed enforcer. Feared by all for her deadly accurate flying slipper.
*   **George (The Gentleman)**: A charming former banker. Quotes poetry and always has a spare handkerchief.
*   **Lorenzo (The Lover)**: A former soap star who checks his reflection in every spoon and name-drops celebrities.
*   **Marge (The Detective)**: A retired cop who squints at everyone and takes notes in a tiny book.

### 🏥 The Staff (NPCs)
*   **Margaret (Head Nurse)**: Speaks VERY... SLOWLY... AND... LOUDLY. 
*   **Gerald (Fitness Coordinator)**: Energetic, wears neon, and blows a whistle for impromptu "Chair Cardio."
*   **Frank (The Handyman)**: Mumbles incoherently and moves at the speed of a turtle in molasses.
*   **Diane (Front Desk)**: Obsessed with her sign-in sheet and "the way things used to be."
