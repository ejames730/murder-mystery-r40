## 🖨️ Printing Tips

### Paper Choices
- **Character sheets**: Cream or beige cardstock (8.5×11")
- **Tracking sheets**: Regular white or cream paper
- **Map**: Large format (11×17" if possible) or standard
- **Tokens**: Cardstock or substitute with poker chips
- **Certificates**: Fancy certificate paper or parchment

### Color vs. B&W
- **Color recommended**: Character sheets, map, certificates
- **B&W acceptable**: Tracking sheets, sign-in sheet, medication log

### Finishing Touches
- Crumple and smooth out papers for "aged" look
- Use coffee or tea staining for vintage effect
- Laminate the map for durability
- Punch holes in tracking sheets for binder clips

## 💡 Customization Ideas
